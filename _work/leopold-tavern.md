Southsea's original craft beer pub, the Leopold Tavern is famous for its ever-changing real ale and bottled beer range. Organisation and planning were the key aspects of my role, as you could never be too prepared for how busy the Leopold could get and how demanding its regulars could be!

![Leopold Tavern](http://leopoldtavern.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/leopoldtavern.jpg "Leopold Tavern")

The Leopold has ten handpumps for cask ale and in a typical week an order could be for over thirty different firkins. As well as maintaining order with the tapped barrels and cellar stock and being ever ready to clean a line this clearly required very broad and deep product knowledge and therefore training for new staff.

A hangout for brewers and expert enthusiasts, the Leopold does tremendous trade for a pub with no kitchen. Very much the leaders in the now huge Portsmouth craft beer scene, multiple local CAMRA awards and the coveted number one spot on Tripadvisor ensured pride amongst the team.
