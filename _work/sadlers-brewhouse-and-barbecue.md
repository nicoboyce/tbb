From the ashes of the Rockstone sister pub Marshalls was born Sadler's - a collaboration between Chris Sadler's midlands brewery and Max and Tom's Barbecue Crew. The bar-restaurant is known for its exquisite smoked meats, its range of traditional ales and an enormous choice of premium and craft gins, bourbons and rums.

I joined the Sadler's team as temporary bar staff but worked as a supervisor with the management team somewhat short-handed. Sadler's was a great opportunity to learn about American barbecue cuisine and also to soak up as much of bar manager Sarah's gin expertise as I could!

At Sadler's Brewhouse & Barbecue, the managers have built a knowledgeable and loyal team which was fun to be a part of. Cellar management was a major feature of my role, with high volume sales of the wide range of ales and also Czech lagers there was a need to really keep on top of the cellar work.
